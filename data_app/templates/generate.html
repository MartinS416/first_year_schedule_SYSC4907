How It Works

1

Build Blocks

Creates blocks of ~20 students for each program based on enrollment numbers. Each block gets a fall and winter term.

2

Prioritize Courses

Shared courses (required by many programs) are scheduled first. Courses with fewer available sections are given higher priority to ensure feasibility.

3

Assign Sections

For each course, the algorithm tries to assign a non-conflicting section (lecture + lab/tutorial) to every block that needs it, respecting room capacities.

4

Kick & Repair

When conflicts arise, the system can displace easier-to-reschedule courses and attempt to re-place them elsewhere — a "kick and repair" strategy.

5

Rank & Report

After generation, each block is scored 0–100 based on schedule quality: penalizing large gaps between classes and insufficient overnight rest between consecutive days.
